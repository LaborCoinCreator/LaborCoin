# LaborCoin (LABR)

## Overview

LaborCoin (LABR) is the native ERC-20 token of the LaborCoin ecosystem.

LABR is designed to support worker organization, collective funding, democratic governance, and strike assistance through an integrated DAO and treasury system.

The token serves four primary functions:

1. Governance participation
2. Treasury funding
3. Dividend distribution
4. Exchange liquidity

## Ecosystem Components

LABR interacts with several protocol contracts:

- LaborCoin Exchange V2
- LaborCoin Governance V12
- LaborCoin Registration V3
- LaborVote V6
- LaborCoin Treasury Module

---

## Transaction Tax System

LaborCoin uses a sell-side taxation model.

Current deployed tax configuration:

| Tax Type | Rate |
|-----------|--------|
| Treasury Tax | 5% |
| Dividend Tax | 5% |
| Burn Tax | 0% |
| Total Sell Tax | 10% |

Taxes are only applied during qualifying sell transactions through the exchange mechanism.

---

## Treasury Allocation

Treasury tax revenue is routed to the protocol treasury.

Treasury funds may be allocated through DAO governance for approved purposes, including:

- Strike assistance
- Worker support initiatives
- Protocol development
- Community-approved funding proposals

---

## Dividend Distribution

Dividend tax revenue is accumulated and distributed through the LaborVote dividend system.

Eligible participants may claim distributions according to the rules defined by the governance framework and dividend contracts.

---

## Governance Utility

LABR holders may participate in governance through the LaborCoin DAO.

Governance functions include:

- Proposal creation
- Proposal voting
- Treasury allocation decisions
- Protocol parameter governance

Governance rules are defined in the Governance V12 contract and associated governance documentation.

---

## Decentralization

The long-term objective of LaborCoin is full community governance through decentralized ownership and DAO-controlled administration.

Ownership transfers and governance transitions are documented within the protocol governance documentation.

---

## Disclaimer

LABR is intended as a governance and participation token within the LaborCoin ecosystem.

Users should review all protocol documentation, smart contracts, and governance materials before participating.
