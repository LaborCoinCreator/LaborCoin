# LaborCoin Governance V13

## Address

`DEPLOYMENT_PENDING`

---

## Purpose

LaborCoin Governance V13 manages proposal creation, voting, and treasury allocation decisions.

Registered LABRV holders participate directly in governance and may propose or vote on treasury distributions according to the rules defined by the governance system.

Governance authority is intentionally limited to treasury allocation decisions and does not extend to protocol administration.

---

## Governance Activation

The governance system activates after a minimum number of registered members has been reached.

This requirement helps ensure that governance begins with meaningful community participation rather than a small number of early adopters.

## Proposal Scope

Governance proposals are limited to treasury distributions.

Approved proposals may direct treasury funds toward activities such as:

* Strike support
* Worker mutual aid
* Labor organizing efforts
* Community-approved initiatives
* Other purposes permitted by the treasury rules

Governance cannot:

* Modify protocol rules
* Change contract logic
* Upgrade contracts
* Alter voting parameters
* Mint additional tokens
* Increase token supply
* Modify exchange behavior
* Modify registration requirements

---

## Voting Rights

Voting power is derived from LABRV.

LABRV is issued to registered members and is non-transferable.

The governance system is designed to reflect participation in the LaborCoin community rather than market-based accumulation.

---

## Security Model

Governance proposals execute through the LaborCoin treasury architecture and remain constrained by predefined protocol limits.

The governance system is intentionally restricted to treasury allocation decisions and cannot exercise administrative control over other deployed LaborCoin contracts.

---

## Immutability

LaborCoin Governance V13 is designed to operate within predefined and transparent rules.

Governance may determine how treasury resources are allocated, but governance itself cannot alter the underlying protocol architecture after deployment.

This separation ensures that treasury decision-making remains democratic while core protocol behavior remains predictable and publicly verifiable.

---

## Role in LaborCoin

The governance system serves as the democratic coordination layer of the LaborCoin ecosystem.

Its purpose is to:

* Facilitate treasury allocation decisions
* Enable community participation
* Coordinate collective funding initiatives
* Support transparent and accountable decision-making

Governance is intentionally limited in scope so that community decision-making can occur without introducing centralized administrative authority over the broader protocol.
